Treasure Trove of Biodiversity

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands, an archipelago of over 500 islands in the Bay of Bengal, host tropical rainforests, mangroves, coral reefs, and beaches that support an incredible variety of plant and animal life — many found nowhere else.

Their isolation fostered remarkable endemism, making them a living laboratory for evolution and a paradise for naturalists.
